## We're here to make wedding planning less overwhelming

Veil to Voyage is a planning resource for newly engaged couples who want
real advice — not just pretty pictures.

We cover everything from your first vendor bookings to your honeymoon
packing list, with honest budget breakdowns and practical guides that
actually help you make decisions.

### What you'll find here

- **Wedding planning checklists** — month-by-month timelines so nothing
  slips through the cracks
- **Honeymoon guides** — destinations by budget, resort reviews, and
  packing lists
- **Registry comparisons** — honest side-by-sides of Zola, The Knot,
  Honeyfund, and Amazon
- **Budget tools** — real cost breakdowns and a free budget spreadsheet
